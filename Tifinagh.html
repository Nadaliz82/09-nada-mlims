International Standardization (Unicode)

Prior to Unicode standardization , Tifinagh was only supported by a Windows ANSI compatible encoding replacing in older (now obsolete) typefaces the letters of the basic Latin alphabet (in this older encoding supported loaded by a conversion utility for Windows provided free of charge by IRCAM, only part of the base Tifinagh was represented, and no difference is made between upper and lower case Latin letters to represent the other missing Tifinagh letters):
